# Airborn Exploration

A Minecraft 1.21.1 (NeoForge) modpack centered on building airships with **Create: Aeronautics** and using them to explore overhauled overworld, Nether, End, and Aether dimensions.

## Gameplay Features

- **Build and fly airships!** Create: Aeronautics and its addons let you assemble flying contraptions powered by Create's kinetic system.
- **Airborn combat!** Create: Big Cannons arms your ships, and Create: Structures Arise scatters pillager airships, sky villages, and airdrops across the world for you to raid.
- **Explore the Aether!** The Aether is restored as a full upper dimension, complete with custom physics for its new blocks so floating builds behave like they should.
- **Expanded overworld and beyond!** Reshape the base dimensions into exciting exploring terrain that you can see all at once from the comfort of your airship.
- **Automation and computers!** Write custom airship control systems in ComputerCraft's fancy lua interface.
- **Survival and food!** Discover a plethora of new food and drink items.
- **Custom recipes and items!** Additional custom items and tweaked recipes to tie the modpack together.
